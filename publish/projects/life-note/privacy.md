# Life Note Privacy Policy

Life Note respects your privacy.

Your notes are stored locally on your device. If you choose to enable GitHub sync, your notes may be uploaded to the GitHub repository or account you configure.

Life Note does not operate its own server to collect or store your notes.

We do not sell your personal data.

If you contact us by email, we may use your email address only to respond to your inquiry.
