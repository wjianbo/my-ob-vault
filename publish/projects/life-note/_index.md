# Life Note

A lightweight life log app for capturing your everyday moments.

Life Note helps you quickly record what happens throughout your day — a meal, a walk, a workout, a thought, a small task, or anything you want to remember.

No heavy diary templates.  
No complicated productivity system.  
Just a simple timeline of your life, one note at a time.
